About Us

A society oriented around the field of data science with its main objectives to be:
(1) informing and training students on the growing field of data science,
(2) guiding members to their first step towards a career in the field, and
(3) providing a platform connect with like-minded data science enthusiasts.

We Have

During the year we will be organising a variety of different events to introduce you to Data Science.

  • You will learn new and train existing data science skills through our biweekly Python workshops
  • You will have the opportunity to network with other data science enthusiasts in our social events
  • You can hear professional data scientists share their passion in our industrial talk and data science at work and seek for internship, placement and graduate job opportunities
  • You will be able to use your professional knowlegde to influence local community through our outreach volunteering opportunity
  • You will hear alumni and current students who worked as data science interns, university research interns and placement job holders sharing their experience in our internship panel
  • Expand your skillset further with our free DataQuest subscription
  • You can show off your knowledge and get a feeling of working as data scientists in our annual Datathon competition
  • You can also be part of our sports team
  • BUT most of all you will become a part of a community which will certainly fascinate you and help you expand your horizons
